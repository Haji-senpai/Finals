ANIMATION


Animation (ANI) Strand provides students with opportunities to express their visual creativity through drawing and storytelling with the aid of computer technology. ANI focuses on Figure and Freehand drawing, traditional animation principles, digital illustration techniques as well as 2D and 3D methodology.



Why should you pick the animation strand? Creativity and imagination combine in the field of animation. If you've always enjoyed creative pursuits such as drawing, illustration, film, and design then you may want to study animation. A degree in this subject gives you the technical and creative skills to enjoy a thriving career in the arts and digital sphere.



What is the benefit of animation course? It opens up the door to boundless creative possibilities. The course covers a wide range of options and methods for producing animation sequences, such as 2D hand-drawing, 2D and 3D computer-generated, stop-motion, and model-based animation approaches.
